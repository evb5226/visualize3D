Why Visualize3D?

Studies show that over 1/3 of students in STEM fields at the university level are kinesthetic learners, but despite the studies most calculus classes are taught only with lectures and notes on a chalkboard. Visualize3D puts multivariable equations right into students hands. Students can see and feel how partial formulas come together to great 3D surfaces. They can feel the peaks and toughs of formulas. To the Visualize3D, it only makes sense to make 3D formulas into 3D objects!
